Cynergy - Collaborate, Innovate, Compete.

"Talk is cheap. show me the code."
- Linus Torvalds

This is a platform that promotes learning and development of skills vital for programming and beyond. A society, where programmers can sense the spirit of camaraderie among their peers.

Cynergy aims to develop a healthy coding culture at RUAS. To achieve this, we plan to bring about an environment where students compete with their peers to earn the title of being the best, using competitive programming as the platfrom. Skills that are forged in this atmosphere are valuable and essential for a programmer.

Competing with others is not the only way to become a programmer. Problem solving skills and the ability to work in teams is vital as well. Realizing this, we aim to be a platform where students can get involved in social coding session, where they can contribute or group together to start a project. We also encourage students to participate in hackathons and competitions, furthering their experience.
